Learning objective one: Understand your car

There are many things on a car for driver to control the movement and check the status of the car: pedals, shift, lights, dash board, safety belt...

When trying to memorize the functions and signs of things on your car, master the following two skills may help you.

  • Get on the car, make the car move a little, and then get off

    • Remember to use the safety belt. Just pretend drive along a street. Try to get familiar with pedals and speed shift.

  • Use the lights

    • Practice how to use lights when at night or facing different weather and emergence situation.
