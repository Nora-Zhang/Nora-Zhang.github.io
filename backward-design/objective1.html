Learning objective one: Understand your car

There are many things on a car for driver to control the movement and check the status of the car: pedals, shift, lights, dash board, safety belt...

When trying to memorize the functions of pedals and steering wheel, as well as the signs on dash board, master the following two skills may help you.

Related Skills

  • Making the car move

    • Remember to use the safety belt. Just pretend driving along a street. Try to get familiar with pedals and speed shift.

  • Using the lights

    • Practice how to use lights when at night or facing different weather and emergence situation.
