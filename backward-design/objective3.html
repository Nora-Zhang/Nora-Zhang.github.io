Learning objective three: Get onto the road

After you know enough about your driving and your car, it is time to keep an eye on other factors of traffic: the environment, other cars and people, weather, signs on the road...

Although there are other things to consider when actually driving, the following two skills are necessary.

Related Skills

  • Changing lane

    • There are several things to notice, such as the distance between you and other cars. Also, try not to change lane near place like a cross.

  • Recognizing signs

    • The signs here including the traffic signs and the marking lines on the road. Make sure you get the information that they indicate.
