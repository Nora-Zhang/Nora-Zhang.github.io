Learning objective two: Control speed and direction

It is an important lesson for novice to be able to control the speed and direction of a car. You may need time and practice.

These two skills can be practical in real life and helpful to understand how steering wheel and pedals can affect the car.

Related Skills

  • Reversing into a parking spot

    • This skill requires precise control of the car, and can be difficult at the beginning. Repeat it for a number of times.

  • Driving along a bridge

    • When going uphill or downhill, the car can be less or more easy to move than usual. Be sure to notice how the slope can influence a car's movement.
